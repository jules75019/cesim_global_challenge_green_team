Option A — Enter the electric market
We currently hold 0% of the electric segment, while RED CAR (44%) and PINK TEAM (25%) are already dominant. Given that electric demand has grown continuously across all regions and infrastructure coverage is forecast to reach 70 to 80% globally by Round 12, entering now represents a strategic growth window before the market fully consolidates.
Investment required$8 to 12M R&D per round
Target marketsUSA & China
Execution riskMedium to High
★
Option B — Intensify hydrogen
We hold 100% of the global hydrogen market and hydrogen infrastructure is still accelerating — reaching 67% in the USA and 52% in Europe by Round 12. Doubling down deepens our structural moat, locks out competitors, and reinforces our position as the undisputed leader in the highest-growth, lowest-competition segment.
Investment required$3 to 5M R&D per round
Target marketsUSA (monopoly) + Europe
Execution riskLow
↓
Option C — Phase out combustion
Combustion demand is declining globally and contribution margins are near zero in most markets. The production and feature costs required to remain competitive are no longer justified by the returns. A phased exit over 3 rounds frees capacity and budget for hydrogen and hybrid — the two segments with the strongest long-term trajectory.
Cost savings$12 to 18M per round
Timeline3-round phased exit
Execution riskLow
